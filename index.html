mdi Houston Branch Grand Opening!

To better serve our customers, Manufacturers Distributor, Inc. (mdi) is proud to announce the opening of our Houston Branch. The new facility is the second warehouse in North America for mdi which operates in Tampa, Florida; the corporate headquarters.

"This second warehouse greatly enhances our ability to provide our customers with the Transair Aluminum Pipe products they want, when they want them", says Sam Arledge, President of mdi. "It also shows our commitment to improving our already exceptional customer service. We will continue to invest in infrastructure and logistics to maximize our capability to perform as the leading distributor of Transair Aluminum Pipe products", continues Arledge.

This state-of-the-art office and warehouse is conveniently located in the Energy Corridor in Katy, Texas to better service our customers by providing shorter shipping times as well as increasing inventory and product availability to the Gulf Coast region and surrounding areas of the U.S. Other quality products distributed by mdi include industrial fire fighting equipment, oil-less air compressors, vacuum pumps, electric and hydraulic winches, electric motors, hose reels, tube expanders and pneumatic tools.

Contact mdi in Houston at 281-769-1717 or sales@mdisales.com.
